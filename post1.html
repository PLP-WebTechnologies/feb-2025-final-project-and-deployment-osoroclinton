Materials You'll Need:

  • Wooden boards
  • Hive body and frames
  • Beeswax foundation

Instructions:

Follow these simple steps to assemble your hive:

  1. Measure and cut the wooden boards to size.
  2. Assemble the hive body using nails and wood glue.
  3. Install the frames inside the hive body.
  4. Apply beeswax foundation to the frames to encourage bees to build comb.
